⚙️ Key DAX

Total Profit =
[Total Revenue] - [Total Cost] - [Cancelled Loss] - [Return Loss]

Profit Margin % =
DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Revenue], 0) * 100

ROI % =
DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Cost], 0) * 100
